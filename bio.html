My name is Adrian Kennedy and I am a software developer.

I've come out of a good long many years in film and have decided to make not-too-big a shift into placing programming at the forefront instead of as an added extra.
Film, TV, DVD production all have vast and until recently largely untapped potential for automation and optimization. Over the last 15+ years I've found countless issues with day-to-day video tasks that have benefitted greatly from a passing knowledge of some of the more geeky tools out there. Some completely knackered footage can be saved using rather simple processes - it's just a matter of targeting the problem and preventing the process from damaging any video that is fine.
